Baigan Sabzi

Vegetable dishes are quite common in the world of Indian cuisine. Eggplants, which are also known as baingan in Hindi, are featured in a wide range of dishes. This recipe for baingan aaloo ki subji is really easy and turns out a delicious dish that tastes great when served with hot chapatis.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Eggplant
  • 3 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 large onion
  • cumin powder
  • turmeric powder
  • chili powder

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Prepare the eggplants by washing, patting dry, and cutting into quarters.
  3. Soak the eggplant in warm water.
  4. Sprinkle water on top of the vegetables and stir everything to mix well.
  5. When nearly cooked, remove the cover from the dish and stir.
  6. When done, turn off the heat and then garnish with chopped, fresh coriander leaves. Serve with hot chapatis and a daal dish if you like.
